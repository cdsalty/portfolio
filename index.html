Christopher Soltis

A healthcare professional turned Full-Stack Developer, I have always been intrigued by how things work and how they can be improved. After years of coding as a hobby, I decided to bet on myself and become a Web Developer.

In November 2018, I joined DigitalCrafts and began the transformation from a business professional to a full stack web developer. The process was challenging and intense but so rewarding. I haven’t looked back since.

As a recent graduate, I’m excited about what lays ahead. I look forward to turning ideas into reality and I am fortunate to wake up and do what I love most, code. I have found I have an inclination for working with React however I don’t want to lose touch of the back-end so I am impartial to both.

Outside of work, you can find me spending time with my wife and daughter. We love staying busy, being outside and having fun! I find myself a truly lucky person.
